This is an educational game to practice kanji-based Japanese words.
Click here for more information and source code.

How to play

  • The goal is to find a word that match a kanji given by the game.
  • Type a word before the timer (タイマ) runs out to score some points.
    If you don't know, leave the input field empty and press Enter to skip.
    A new kanji is drawn from the word you typed, or among those of the JLPT level you have selected.
  • If the timer runs out or your answer is invalid, you lose one life (心).
    If you reach 0, it's game over !
  • To get the best score, try to make a "chain", for example 仕事, 事故, 故障, ...
    This will increase the kanji combo multiplier !

Ready ?

To start the game, select your JLPT level:
  • N5 (79 kanjis)
  • N4 (245 kanjis)
  • N3 (612 kanjis)
  • N2 (979 kanjis)
  • N1 (1964 kanjis)
