This is an educational game to practice kanji-based Japanese words.
Click here for more information and source code.

How to play

  • The goal is to find a word that match a kanji given by the game.
  • Type a word in rōmaji and press Enter to validate, before the timer (タイマ) reaches 0.
    If you don't know, leave the input field empty and press Enter to skip.
  • If there is multiple words possible, a box will pop up for you to select which word you meant
  • If your answer is valid, a new kanji will be drawn from the word you typed (or among those of the JLPT level you have selected).
  • If your answer is invalid or you run out of time, you lose one life (心).
    If you reach 0, it's game over !
  • To get the best score (点), try to make a "chain", for example 仕事, 事故, 故障, ...
    This will increase the kanji combo multiplier (コンボ) !

Ready ?

To start the game, select your JLPT level:
  • N5 (79 kanjis)
  • N4 (245 kanjis)
  • N3 (612 kanjis)
  • N2 (979 kanjis)
  • N1 (1964 kanjis)
